Get Help in the UK

If you've been targeted by a scam or need advice, these UK organisations are here to help. Don't hesitate to reach out.

Action Fraud

The UK's national reporting centre for fraud and cybercrime. Report scams here to help the police track down criminals.

Citizens Advice

Offers free, confidential advice online, over the phone, and in person on how to deal with the aftermath of a scam.

National Cyber Security Centre

Part of GCHQ, the NCSC provides expert advice for citizens and businesses on how to stay safe online.
